Card

A card is a flexible and extensible content container with various options like header, footer, powerfull display otions, contextual bg-colors and wide varity of content.

Cards support a wide varity of content including images, text, list groups, links and so on.

  • card-body
    • this is known as building block of a card.
  • card-title
    • used for adding title to a card.
  • card-img-top
    • used to place image on top of card.
  • card-text
    • using this text can be added to card
